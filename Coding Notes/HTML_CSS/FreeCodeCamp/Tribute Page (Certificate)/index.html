Steve Jobs

The man who revolutionalized the industry

Here's a timeline of Steve Jobs life:

  • 1955 - Born in San Francisco, California, EUA and adopted
  • 1971 - Steve Jobs meets Wozniak in college
  • 1972 - Steve Jobs enrolls in Reed College, but drops out
  • 1976 - Wozniak and Jobs created Apple with Ronald Wayne in Job's garage
  • 1978 - Job's girlfriend, Brennan gave birth to Lisa. Jobs denies paternity at first
  • 1984 - Apple introduces the Machintosh
  • 1985 - Steve Jobs leaves Apple. and founds NeXT
  • 1986 - Jobs founded The Graphics Group, which would later be called Pixar Studios
  • 1989 - Jobs meets his wife, Laurene Powell and proposed in New Year's Day in the following year
  • 1991 - Jobs marries in Yosemite Park and Laurene gave birth to the first child, Reed
  • 1995 - Laurene gave birth to the second child, Erin
  • 1996 - Apple announced plans to buy NeXT (Jobs company) for $247 million and Steve Jobs comes back to Apple
  • 1997 - Steve Jobs became the CEO of Apple in September
  • 1998 - Apple introduces the iMac
  • 2001 - Apple introduces the iPod for $399 and sells more than 100 000 iPods
  • 2003 - Steve Jobs was diagnosed with pancreatic cancer
  • 2006 - Disney buys Pixar for $7.4 billion, he became the largest shareholder in the Walt Disney Company with 7%
  • 2007 - Apple launches the first iPhone, which sold 3.7 million
  • 2010 - Apple releases the iPad with 16 to 64GB, which cost $499 to $829
  • 2011 - Jobs leaves Apple, this time for good
  • 2011 - Steve Jobs died of pancreatic cancer
